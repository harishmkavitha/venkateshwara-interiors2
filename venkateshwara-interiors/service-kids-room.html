Good To Know

Kids Room — your questions, answered.

Are the materials used safe for children?

Yes — we use low-VOC, child-safe finishes, rounded edges and secure fittings throughout kids’ rooms.

Can the room adapt as my child grows?

We design with growth in mind — adjustable desks, modular storage and timeless bases that just need restyling later, not rebuilding.

Can you do a shared room for two children?

Absolutely — bunk beds, twin study zones and divided storage make shared rooms work smoothly.
