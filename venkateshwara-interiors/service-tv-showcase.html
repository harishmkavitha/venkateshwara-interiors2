Good To Know

TV Showcase — your questions, answered.

Can you hide all the cables and devices?

Yes — concealed conduits and ventilated cabinets keep wires and gadgets completely out of view while staying accessible.

Can the unit double as storage?

Definitely. We often combine the TV unit with drawers, display niches, bookshelves or a small bar.

Will it suit a wall-mounted TV?

Whether wall-mounted or on a console, we design the backdrop and unit to suit your screen size and seating distance.
