Good To Know

Toilet & Bathroom — your questions, answered.

How important is waterproofing, really?

It’s the single most important step. We use proven membranes and correct floor slopes so you never face seepage or damage later.

Can you renovate a bathroom without a full demolition?

In many cases, yes — surface renovations and refits are possible. We’ll assess and recommend the least disruptive route.

Do you provide the sanitaryware and fittings?

We can supply and install branded sanitaryware and CP fittings, or work with pieces you’ve chosen — your call.
